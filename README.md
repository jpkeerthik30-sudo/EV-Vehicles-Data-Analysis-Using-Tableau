# EV-Vehicles-Data-Analysis-Using-Tableau

##  Project Overview:
This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) population data to uncover trends in adoption, manufacturer dominance, and geographic distribution. The analysis is visualized using an interactive Tableau dashboard.

---

##  Objectives:
- Analyze growth trends of electric vehicles over time
  
- Compare BEV vs PHEV adoption
  
- Identify leading EV manufacturers
  
- Study geographic distribution of EVs
  
- Evaluate CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility

---

## Dataset
- Source: Electric Vehicle Population Data
  
- Total Records: 150,413 vehicles
  
- Data includes:
  
  - Model year
  - Manufacturer
  - EV type (BEV/PHEV)
  - Electric range
  - Location
  - CAFV eligibility

---

##  Key Metrics:

- **Total Vehicles:** 150,413
  
- **Average Electric Range:** 67.83 miles
  
- **BEV Vehicles:** 116,745 (77.6%)
  
- **PHEV Vehicles:** 33,668 (22.4%)

---

##  Vehicle Trend Analysis:
- EV adoption increased significantly after 2016
  
- Peak growth observed in 2022 and 2023
  
- Slight drop in 2024 due to incomplete data  

---

##  Geographic Distribution:
- EV adoption is higher in states with:
  
 - Strong infrastructure
    
 - Government incentives
    
 - Uneven distribution across regions  

---

## 🏭 Top Manufacturers:

- **Tesla** dominates with ~52% market share  
- Other key players:
  - Nissan  
  - Chevrolet  
  - Ford  
  - BMW  
- Top 10 manufacturers contribute most EVs  

---

##  CAFV Eligibility Analysis:

- Eligible: 41.81%
  
- Not Eligible: 11.85%
  
- Unknown: 46.34%  

---

##  Model-Level Insights:
- Tesla dominates BEV category
  
- BMW and Audi lead PHEV segment
    
- Many smaller models contribute to a long-tail distribution  

---

## Tableau Dashboard Features:
- Interactive filtering by:
  - State
    
  - EV type
    
  - Manufacturer
    
- Visualizations include:
  - Trend charts
    
  - Maps
     
  - Manufacturer comparison  

---

##  Key Insights:
- EV adoption is rapidly increasing
  
- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) dominate the market
  
- Market leadership is highly concentrated
  
- Geographic disparities highlight infrastructure gaps  

---

##  Tools Used:
- Tableau (Dashboard & Visualization)
  
- Excel / CSV (Data Source)  

---

##  Conclusion:
Electric vehicles are becoming mainstream, driven by technological advancement and policy support. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of trends and helps in data-driven decision-making.
